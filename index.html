F.A.Q

  • What is a MUN ?

    It is an attempt to simulate the real world United Nations, wherein different Councils of the UN are simulated, with pressing agendas. Diplomats debate and discuss about the agenda allocated to them

  • What do I do at a MUN?

    You can opt in as a delegate, who will represent a certain country and depict their foreign policy at the committee or you can be a press personnel, who perform reporting or photography. The choice is yours.

  • Would you be providing accommodation to outstationed delegates?

    Yes , Accomodation will be provided
